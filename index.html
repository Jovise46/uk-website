Local Tips

  • Kelvingrove, Riverside Museum & Glasgow Cathedral are all free — budget for food instead.
  • Book Margo and The Buttery at least a week in advance — they fill fast.
  • The SPT Subway (the "Clockwork Orange") runs a loop — perfect for West End to City Centre hops.
  • Glaswegians are famously friendly — don't hesitate to ask locals for recommendations.
  • Bring a waterproof layer regardless of season. The weather changes fast.
  • Buchanan Street is fully pedestrianised — great for an evening stroll and people-watching.
